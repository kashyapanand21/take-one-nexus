How can I earn credits?
Credits are awarded for active participation in the Nexus:
• Uploading quality portfolio work and scripts
• Successful collaborations on film sets
• Active messaging and networking
• Completing your profile to 100%
• Community contributions and moderation
Where can I see my credits?
Your current credit balance is displayed on your personal Profile page, here on the global Leaderboard, and on your Dashboard indicators when logged in.
